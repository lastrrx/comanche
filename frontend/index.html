Getting Started with Cypher

1. Connect Your Wallet

Click the "Connect Wallet" button in the top right corner and select your preferred Solana wallet (Phantom, Solflare, or Backpack).

2. View Your Portfolio

Once connected, your portfolio will automatically load showing your token holdings, values, and performance metrics.

3. Explore Features

Navigate through different sections using the tabs below to access analytics, market data, whale tracking, alerts, and social features.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Cypher free to use?

Yes, Cypher is completely free to use. No subscriptions or hidden fees.

Which wallets are supported?

Cypher supports Phantom, Solflare, and Backpack wallets for Solana.

How often is data updated?

Portfolio and market data is updated in real-time, with price feeds refreshing every 30 seconds.

Is my wallet data secure?

Cypher only reads public blockchain data. We never access private keys or make transactions.
