Help & Tutorial

Welcome to Cypher!

Your advanced Solana portfolio analytics platform. Here's how to get started:

  1. Connect Your Wallet: Click the "Connect Wallet" button and select your preferred Solana wallet.
  2. Explore Your Portfolio: View your holdings, performance charts, and risk analysis.
  3. Set Up Alerts: Create price alerts to stay informed about market movements.
  4. Follow Traders: Discover and follow successful traders to learn from their strategies.
  5. Track Achievements: Unlock achievements as you trade and use the platform.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my wallet safe with Cypher?

Yes! Cypher only reads your public wallet data. We never store or access your private keys.

How often is data updated?

Portfolio and market data is updated in real-time. Price feeds are refreshed every few seconds.

Can I use multiple wallets?

Currently, you can connect one wallet at a time. Multi-wallet support is coming soon!
